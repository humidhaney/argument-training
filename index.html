A workshop for picking apart bad arguments.

This is a training room for three skills: spotting logical fallacies in the wild, countering bad arguments without sounding like a pedantic logic nerd, and steel-manning positions you disagree with so your own thinking gets sharper.

Sixty fallacies across seven categories. Three modes. Examples pulled from a world that looks like yours — small business, New Orleans politics, futures trading, social-media discourse.
